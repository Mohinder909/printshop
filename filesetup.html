Vector Format


Only Vector File Format for printing! Any other format will distort the desired image on the business card. And we do not compromise with the quality to bestow you the ultimate business card. The Bitmap Formats such as jpg, png, jpeg, bmp, psd, tiff are not recommended for printing!


Don’t have a vector format of your logo? Don’t worry! We provide you the logo tracing service to provide you the best service.


We accept the following file format: –


  1. .cdr
  2. EPS
  3. PDF
  4. Ai
